# Experiment 005 Protocol — Incremental Outcome Information in Frozen Abnormal-Action Residuals

Status: **preregistered before outcome execution**.

## Question

After a bookmaker-action residual has become observable, does it add locked-test match-outcome information beyond the market probability available at that same time?

This is an incremental-information test. It is not yet an expected-value, staking, limit or profitability test.

## Timing rule

A residual labeled `T-h` uses the realized target-book action from state `t` to state `t+1`. Therefore it is first observable one hour after `t`.

The market comparator is reconstructed at `t+1`:

`target_next_probability = target_current_probability + actual_delta`

The direct market probability for a match/cutoff is the normalized mean of those `t+1` target-book probabilities across eligible frozen books. The stale market at `t` must not be used as the main comparator.

## Frozen sources

1. Phase 19 validation/test residual files generated by the promoted Experiment 002 hazard and Experiment 003 conditional-movement architectures.
2. `score_home` and `score_away` from the two acquired Beat The Bookie odds-series files, joined only after residual generation.

The residual generator contains no result/score access.

## Unit and eligibility

Unit: one `match_id × cutoff` record.

Frozen cutoffs: T-48h, T-24h, T-12h, T-6h, T-3h, T-1h. The information timestamp is one hour after each labeled cutoff.

Aggregate across the same eight frozen bookmaker slots used in Experiments 001–003. No bookmaker is selected using outcomes.

Require at least four unique eligible frozen-book rows in a match/cutoff record. Duplicate match/book/cutoff rows are a hard error.

## Split

- Outcome-layer training: residual **validation** period only (2016-07-01 through 2016-08-31).
- Locked evaluation: residual **test** period only (2016-09-01 onward).

The original normal-behavior training period is not reused as outcome training because outcome-blind residuals were frozen only for validation/test.

## Outcome

Three classes:

- 0: home win (`score_home > score_away`)
- 1: draw
- 2: away win

Conflicting scores for the same match ID are a hard error.

## Direct market reference

Normalized mean `t+1` de-vigged H/D/A probability across eligible frozen books.

No fitting.

## Fitted market-only baseline

Fixed multinomial logistic regression:

- features: clipped log direct-market H/D/A probabilities plus six cutoff one-hot indicators
- `StandardScaler`
- `LogisticRegression(C=1.0, solver="lbfgs", max_iter=500)`
- no validation/test hyperparameter search

## Frozen augmented residual features

The augmented model receives exactly the same market/cutoff features plus:

- book count and actual mover fraction
- predicted move-probability mean
- move-surprise signed mean, standard deviation, sum and maximum absolute value
- no-move-surprise mean/maximum
- unexpected-move-surprise mean/maximum
- for each H/D/A unconditional action residual: mean, standard deviation, sum, maximum absolute value and positive-sign fraction
- action-residual L2 mean, standard deviation and maximum
- for each H/D/A conditional residual among actual movers: mean
- conditional-residual L2 mean/maximum
- consensus-gap L2 mean
- prior residual-cutoff count mean
- prior move-surprise mean
- prior absolute move-surprise mean
- prior action-residual L2 mean
- prior abnormality mean
- mean prior cumulative H/D/A action residual

Undefined mover-conditional aggregates are set to zero; mover fraction remains present so zero is distinguishable from observed mover averages.

Fixed augmented model: the same scaler/logistic architecture and `C=1.0` as the market-only baseline.

## Metrics

Primary: multiclass log loss.

Secondary:

- multiclass Brier score, defined as mean summed squared error across H/D/A
- accuracy
- per-cutoff log loss/Brier
- predicted class-probability means versus observed frequencies

## Uncertainty

Paired bootstrap over match IDs, 1,000 fixed-seed replicates. All cutoff rows for a sampled match remain together.

Primary bootstrap estimand: fitted-market-only log loss minus augmented log loss.

## Promotion rule

Residual outcome information is promoted only if on locked test:

1. augmented log loss is lower than fitted market-only log loss;
2. paired match-bootstrap 95% CI for baseline-minus-augmented log loss is entirely above zero;
3. augmented multiclass Brier is lower than fitted market-only Brier;
4. augmented log loss improves at least 4 of 6 cutoffs;
5. augmented log loss is also lower than the unfitted direct `t+1` market reference.

All five conditions are required.

A failure is valid. No feature deletion, threshold search or alternate model may be selected after viewing locked-test results.

## Follow-on gate

Only if Experiment 005 passes may a separate preregistered economic study test executable prices, vig, closing-line value and return. Outcome information alone is not profitability.
