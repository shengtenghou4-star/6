# Figure captions

## Figure 1 — Fixed-identity abnormal-action residual design

`figures/residual_pipeline.svg`

The contemporaneous market state feeds both a normal-action model and a raw future-price model. The observed action minus expected action forms the abnormal-action residual. The raw model fixes bookmaker and outcome identity; residual information may rerank only that same candidate. Future same-book closing-price quality is the primary mechanism target. Realized return and execution are separate downstream conversion layers.
